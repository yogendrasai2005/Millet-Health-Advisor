Scientific Evidence & Research

Backed by peer-reviewed studies and nutritional science

Vector Database (RAG)

Our system queries a ChromaDB vector store populated with scientific literature and nutritional datasets. It uses semantic search to retrieve context-aware evidence regarding millet efficacy for specific health conditions (e.g., Diabetes, Anemia).

Nutritional Analysis

Quantitative analysis of macro and micro-nutrient profiles. The system calculates suitability scores based on specific bioactive compounds (Calcium, Iron, Fiber) to ensure recommendations align with biochemical health goals.

User Experiences

A Natural Language Processing engine analyzes 781+ verified consumer reviews. It classifies feedback into positive, neutral, and negative clusters to predict user satisfaction regarding taste, texture, and digestibility.
